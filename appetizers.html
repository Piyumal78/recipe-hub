1. Garlic Bread Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Parsley and cheese (optional)

Steps:

  1. Mix butter and minced garlic together.
  2. Spread on bread slices and bake at 180Â°C for 10 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle cheese and parsley, then serve warm.

2. Mini Vegetable Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • Spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, cabbage, beans)
  • Soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Stir-fry the vegetables with soy sauce and pepper.
  2. Fill the wrappers with the mixture and roll tightly.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown and serve with sauce.
