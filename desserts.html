Dessert Recipes

1. Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup whipped cream

Steps:

  1. Melt the chocolate and let it cool slightly.
  2. Beat egg yolks and mix into the chocolate.
  3. Whisk egg whites with sugar until stiff peaks form.
  4. Fold in whipped cream and egg whites gently.
  5. Refrigerate for 2 hours before serving.
